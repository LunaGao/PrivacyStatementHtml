We collect your personal information primarily for the following purposes:

3. Content Publishing and Visibility

3.1 Public Content

Content you post (including text, images, audio, video, etc.) is visible to other users by default. Please note:

  • Your username and posted content will be displayed to other users

3.2 Content Deletion Restrictions

In certain circumstances, content you post may not be deletable:
